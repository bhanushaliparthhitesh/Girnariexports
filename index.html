Welcome to Girnari Export

Welcome to Girnari Export, the leading onion powder exporter based in India. For over three decades, our name has been synonymous with premium quality, delivering the authentic, robust flavor of Indian onions to over 70 countries worldwide. We understand that global kitchens demand the absolute best, which is why we meticulously source our raw materials directly from dedicated farmers, ensuring a farm-to-table freshness that is unmatched in the industry.

As a fully certified exporter, our rigorous quality control processes guarantee that every batch of our food grade onion powder meets strict international standards. Whether you are a large-scale food manufacturer seeking bulk export solutions, or a gourmet distributor in search of organic variants, Girnari Export is your trusted partner. Our state-of-the-art dehydration and milling technology preserves the essential oils and aroma of the onions, resulting in a consistent flavor profile that chefs and food technologists rely on.

We pride ourselves on our certified operations, holding accreditations from FSSAI, FDA, ISO, and more, which reflect our unwavering commitment to food safety and hygiene. By combining advanced processing techniques with our deep-rooted agricultural expertise, we provide an extensive product range that caters to diverse culinary applications. From flavoring gourmet snacks to enriching savory soups and sauces, our premium quality onion powder enhances every dish. When you choose Girnari Export, you are partnering with a globally recognized onion powder exporter dedicated to excellence, reliability, and unparalleled flavor.
