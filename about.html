About Girnari Export | India's Trusted Onion Powder Supplier

Discover the rich heritage and dedication that drives our company forward.

Girnari Export began with a simple yet ambitious vision: to bring the authentic, rich flavors of Indian agriculture to the global stage. As a premier onion powder company in India, our roots are deeply intertwined with the fertile soils and traditional farming communities of our region. For over 35 years, we have grown from a modest local supplier into an internationally recognized export powerhouse.

Our vast export experience allows us to navigate the complex landscape of international food logistics seamlessly. We have built enduring relationships with clients across more than 70 countries, proving time and again that our commitment to quality transcends borders. The cornerstone of our success lies in our unwavering dedication to excellence—from the meticulous selection of raw onions from our trusted network of farmers to the advanced dehydration techniques employed in our state-of-the-art facilities.

We believe that great food starts with exceptional ingredients. That is why our mission is to deliver onion powder that retains its natural aroma, pungency, and nutritional value. We continually invest in modern technology and sustainable practices, ensuring that our operations not only meet the highest hygiene standards but also respect the environment. Our facility operates heavily on renewable energy, showcasing our forward-thinking approach.

As an industry-leading exporter, we are proud of our heritage, our people, and our products. We invite you to explore our certified range and discover why top chefs, manufacturers, and distributors consistently choose Girnari Export.
