Quality Certifications | FSSAI, FDA, ISO Approved

Upholding the strictest global food safety protocols.

At Girnari Export, maintaining uncompromising quality is the bedrock of our business. As a globally recognized onion powder exporter, we strictly adhere to rigorous food safety and hygiene protocols. Our manufacturing facilities are meticulously audited and evaluated to ensure we meet and exceed international quality standards.

We are proud to be FSSAI certified, which affirms that our products comply with India's highest food safety and standards authority regulations. Furthermore, to cater to the stringent demands of our North American clients, our processing units are FDA approved. This guarantees that our onion powder is manufactured under strictly regulated conditions that prioritize consumer safety.

To ensure consistency and operational excellence, we follow comprehensive ISO standards (ISO 22000), implementing a robust Food Safety Management System (FSMS). In addition, our HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point) certification ensures that any potential physical, chemical, or biological hazards are systematically identified and eliminated during the production process.

By prioritizing these certifications, we assure our buyers—whether they are food processors, distributors, or chefs—that they are receiving an ingredient that is not only intensely flavorful but exceptionally safe. We stand behind the integrity of every shipment.
